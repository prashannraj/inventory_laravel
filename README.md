# Full-Stack Inventory Management System - Demonstration Guide

Welcome to the **Inventory Management System**, a comprehensive solution for managing products, sales, purchases, and stock levels. This guide will walk you through the core features and modules of the application.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### 1. Dashboard
The **Dashboard** is your central command center. It provides real-time insights into your business:
- **Financial Widgets**: View Net Revenue, Total Sales, and Active Products.
- **Revenue Chart**: Interactive visualization of your sales performance (localized in **Rs.**).
- **Recent Sales**: A quick glance at the latest transactions.
- **Support**: Direct access to technical support via [appantech.com.np](https://appantech.com.np) or phone at **+9779807669230**.

---

## 📦 Inventory & Products

### 2. Product Management
- **Centralized Catalog**: List, search, and filter your entire inventory.
- **Detailed Profiles**: Each product tracks SKU, Barcode, Category, Brand, Unit, and Tax Rates.
- **Stock Tracking**: View current stock levels with automatic "Low Stock" alerts.
- **Valuation**: The system calculates both Cost and Potential Sale value for your entire inventory.

### 3. Categories & Brands
- Organize products into logical categories and brands for easier management and reporting.

### 4. Attributes
- Manage product variations (e.g., Color, Size) and their specific values.

---

## 💰 Sales & Transactions

### 5. POS Interface (Point of Sale)
- **High-Speed Checkout**: A dedicated interface for quick retail sales.
- **Product Search**: Find items instantly by name or SKU.
- **Cart Management**: Add items, adjust quantities, and apply discounts on the fly.
- **Multi-Payment**: Supports Cash and Card payments with automatic change calculation.

### 6. Sales History
- View itemized invoices for every transaction.
- Track payment statuses (Paid, Partial, Unpaid).

---

## 🛒 Procurement & Suppliers

### 7. Purchase Orders
- **Restock Inventory**: Create purchase orders for suppliers.
- **Stock Integration**: Received items are automatically added to your warehouse stock.
- **Invoice Attachment**: Upload and store PDF/Image invoices for every purchase.

### 8. Supplier Management
- Track supplier contact details, tax numbers, and opening balances.

---

## 🔧 Stock Adjustments

### 9. Inventory Correction
- Manually adjust stock levels for specific reasons:
  - Damaged items
  - Inventory count corrections
  - Returns
- Full history of all adjustments for auditing.

---

## 📊 Reporting

### 10. Financial Reports
- **Sales Reports**: Detailed breakdown of revenue over custom date ranges.
- **Purchase Reports**: Analyze procurement costs and supplier trends.
- **Inventory Reports**: Comprehensive valuation of your current stock on hand.
